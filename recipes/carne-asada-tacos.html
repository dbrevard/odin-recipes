Carne Asada Tacos

Easy carne asada street tacos

Ingredients

  • Flank Steak
  • Vegetable oil
  • Onions
  • Limes, plus extra for toppings
  • Cilantro
  • Salt and pepper
  • Butter
  • mini corn tortillas

Steps

  1. Step One
  2. Thinly slice the steak the chop into bite size pieces. In a skillet over medium high heat, begin to brown the steak in the oil.
  3. Step Two
  4. Add half the onions to the skillet and cook until soft. Save the rest of the onions. Meanwhile season with salt and pepper.
  5. Step Three
  6. Squeeze the juice of two limes over the meat and onion mixture.
  7. Step Four
  8. Continue cooking until the meat is cooked through.
  9. Step Five
  10. While the steak is cooking, melt the butter in another skillet.
  11. Step Six
  12. Fry the mini corn tortillas in the melted butter for 1 minute until they are soft. Allow them to cool slightly.
  13. Step Seven
  14. You will need 2 corn tortillas per taco. Spoon the meat mixture on the tortillas.
  15. Step Eight
  16. Top with cilantro, fresh onions and serve with limes. Salt and pepper to taste.
