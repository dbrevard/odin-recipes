Quesadillas

Easy chicken quesadillas recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 flour tortillas (8")
  • 2 teaspoons butter, melted
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/3 cup finely chopped onions
  • 1 medium tomato, diced
  • 1 package (9oz) frozen diced cooked chicken, thawed and chopped

Steps

  1. Step One
  2. Brush one side of each tortilla with butter.
  3. Step Two
  4. Place two tortillas, buttered side down, on a griddle; sprinkle with cheese, onion, tomato and chicken.
  5. Step Three
  6. Top with remaining tortillas, buttered side up.
  7. Step Four
  8. Cook over medium heat for 3-4 minutes on each side or until lightly browned. Cut into wedges.
