Ramen
Restaurant-style Shoyu Miso Ramen

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup dried black fungus
  • 2 cups mirin
  • 1 1/4 cups soy sauce, divided
  • 1/2 brown sugar
  • 6 green onion bulbs, chopped, divided
  • 1/2 onion, coarsely chopped
  • 6 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 2lbs skin-on, boneless pork belly
  • butcher's twine
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup miso paste
  • 4 (3 ounce) packages ramen noodles
  • 4 sheets nori (dry seaweed), quartered
  • 1 naruto (fish paste stick with a red spiral pattern), sliced

Steps

  1. Step One
  2. Preheat the oven to 275Â°F
  3. Step Two
  4. Place black fungus in a large bowl and fill with water
  5. Step Three
  6. Combine mirin, 1/2 cup soy sauce, 1/2 cup brown sugar, 3 green onion bulbs, chopped onion, and garlic in an oven-safe pot over high heat. Bring to a boil.
  7. Step Four
  8. Place pork belly skin-side down on a flat work surface. Roll up lengthwise and wrap with butcher's twine. Place pork belly in the pot with the mirin mixture and partially cover with a lid.
  9. Step Five
  10. Bake in the preheated oven until pork is tender, about 4 hours.
  11. Step Six
  12. Bring a separate pot of water to a boil over high heat. Gently place eggs in the pot and cook until yolks are barely set, 8 to 10 minutes. Transfer eggs to a bowl filled with ice water. Let sit, about 1 minute. Remove from water and peel eggs.
  13. Step Seven
  14. Place eggs in a container with 1 cup water and 1/2 cup soy sauce. Dampen a paper towel in the mixture and use it to cover the container. Refrigerate 4 hours to overnight.
  15. Step Eight
  16. Drain the fungus and add to the liquid in the pot with the pork belly. Cover with a lid and refrigerate, 4 hours to overnight.
  17. Step Nine
  18. Skim fungus from the top of the pork belly mixture and place in a pot with 8 cups water; fungus should be covered in pork belly fat. Add 1/4 cup soy sauce and miso paste. Bring to a boil.
  19. Step Ten
  20. Remove skin from the pork belly using a knife. Chop the meat into pieces of desired thickness. Cut eggs in half lengthwise.
  21. Step Eleven
  22. Bring a separate pot of water to a boil. Cook ramen in boiling water, stirring occasionally, until noodles are tender, yet firm to the bite, about 3 minutes. Drain.
  23. Step Twelve
  24. Place 4 slices of nori diagonally in the corner of each bowl. Place noodles on top; arrange 2 egg halves and a few pork belly slices in separate corners. Cover with black fungus, top with green onions, and pour in broth. Top each bowl with a few slices of naruto. Let sit, about 3 minutes, before serving.
